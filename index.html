Barcodim Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2025

Barcodim ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. However, third-party services used within the App, such as advertising providers, may collect information from your device.

1. Information We Collect

We, Barcodim, do not collect any personal data, analytics, or tracking information. However, third-party advertising providers integrated into the App may collect:

  • Device Information: Model, OS, unique identifiers.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise location (if permitted).
  • User Behavior: Interaction with ads, app usage time.

2. Third-Party Advertising & Data Collection

While we do not store or process personal data, third-party ad providers (primarily Unity Ads and Google AdMob) may collect and process data according to their policies. We do not control their data usage.

2.1 Unity Ads

Our app integrates Unity Ads for advertising purposes. Unity Ads may collect and process the following information:

  • Device identifiers (like advertising ID, device ID)
  • IP address and general location data
  • Information about your device (model, operating system, network provider)
  • App usage data and ad interaction information

For more information on Unity's data practices, please refer to Unity's Privacy Policy.

2.2 Cloudinary

We use Cloudinary to store and manage user-uploaded product images. When you upload an image in our app:

  • The image is sent to and stored on Cloudinary's servers
  • Images are associated with product barcodes, not with your personal information
  • Images may be publicly accessible to anyone with the correct URL

For more information, please refer to Cloudinary's Privacy Policy.

3. Managing Your Privacy

To limit third-party data collection, you can:

  • Disable personalized ads in device settings.
  • Use a VPN or private network.
  • Manage app permissions in system settings.
  • Reset your advertising ID in your device settings.
  • Opt-out of interest-based advertising through your device settings:
    • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • On iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

5. User-Generated Content (Reviews & Images)

Please be aware of the following regarding content you submit within the App:

  • Reviews and uploaded images are publicly visible to all other users of the app.
  • Once submitted, reviews cannot be deleted by the user, even after clearing data or uninstalling the App.
  • Reviews and uploaded product images will remain stored on our servers and are not affected by local device settings.
  • Product images you upload will be stored on Cloudinary's servers and will be associated with product barcodes.
  • We cannot guarantee that images or reviews will be completely deleted from all third-party servers after removal from our system.

By submitting reviews or images, you grant us a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, process, and display your content for the purpose of providing and improving our services.

6. Clearing Data & Its Limitations

  • The "Clear Data" button removes stored data only from your local device.
  • Clearing data does not delete your reviews or other content stored on our servers.
  • To request permanent removal of content, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers and third-party service providers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage users to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
