Barcodim Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

Barcodim (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. However, third-party services used within the App, such as advertising providers, may collect information from your device.

1. Information We Collect

Barcodim does not collect any personal data, analytics, or tracking information. Nevertheless, the third-party advertising provider integrated into the app—Google AdMob—may collect:

  • Device Information: model, operating-system version, unique identifiers.
  • Location Data: approximate or precise location (if you grant permission).
  • User Behaviour: ad interactions, session duration, and basic app-usage data.

2. Third-Party Advertising & Data Collection

While Barcodim does not store or process personal data, Google AdMob may do so in accordance with its own policies. We do not control how Google processes or shares that information.

2.1 Google AdMob

Our app integrates Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Depending on your device settings and your consent, AdMob may collect and process the following information:

  • Advertising ID and other device identifiers
  • IP address and coarse location data
  • Device details (model, OS version, network provider, language)
  • In-app activity and interaction with ads

For details on Google’s data practices, please see Google AdMob & Ads Privacy Policy.

2.2 Cloudinary

We use Cloudinary to store and manage user-uploaded product images. When you upload an image:

  • The image is sent to and stored on Cloudinary’s servers.
  • Images are associated with product barcodes, not with your personal information.
  • Images may be publicly accessible to anyone with the correct URL.

For more information, see the Cloudinary Privacy Policy.

3. Managing Your Privacy

You can limit third-party data collection by:

  • Disabling personalised ads in your device settings.
  • Using a VPN or private network.
  • Managing app permissions (camera, location, etc.) in system settings.
  • Resetting your advertising ID.
  • Opting out of interest-based advertising:
    • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > “Opt out of Ads Personalisation”
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > disable “Allow Apps to Request to Track”

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

5. User-Generated Content (Reviews & Images)

Please be aware of the following regarding content you submit within the app:

  • Reviews and uploaded images are publicly visible.
  • Once submitted, reviews cannot be deleted by the user.
  • Reviews and images remain on our servers even after you clear local data or uninstall the app.
  • Product images are hosted on Cloudinary and linked to product barcodes.
  • We cannot guarantee that content is completely erased from all third-party caches once removed from our system.

By submitting reviews or images, you grant us a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free licence to use, reproduce, process, and display that content to provide and improve our services.

6. Clearing Data & Its Limitations

  • The “Clear Data” button removes stored data only from your local device.
  • Clearing data does not delete your reviews or content stored on our servers.
  • To request permanent removal of content, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided such information, contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the app signifies acceptance of the revised policy. Please review this page periodically for the latest information.
